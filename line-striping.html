Parking Lot Line Striping — Richmond VA & Central Virginia

Clear, ADA-compliant line striping is a legal requirement for most commercial parking lots — and a major factor in customer experience, traffic flow, and liability management. Faded or missing lines create confusion, increase accident risk, and can result in costly ADA violations. J. Worden & Sons provides professional parking lot striping for businesses, property managers, churches, schools, and commercial properties throughout Richmond VA and all of Central Virginia.

We use professional-grade traffic paint applied with precision striping equipment — crisp, uniform lines that last 2–3 years. Our services include complete re-striping, new layout design, ADA handicap spaces, fire lane marking, directional arrows, numbered spaces, curb painting, and custom stenciling. We also handle restriping after sealcoating, typically the following day after sealer cures.

Parking Lot Line Striping — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does parking lot striping cost in Richmond VA?

$200–$500 for small lots (20–30 spaces), $500–$1,500 for larger lots. New ADA layout adds to cost. Free in-person estimates.

How many ADA spaces are required?

1 per 25 total spaces for lots up to 100 spaces, with 1 van-accessible space per 6 accessible. We design to current ADA Standards.

How long does striping last?

2–3 years under normal traffic. Restripe when lines fade to less than 50% visibility.

When should I restripe after sealcoating?

The following day after sealer fully cures (24–48 hours).

Do you do fire lane striping?

Yes — fire lanes, red curb, no-parking markings per Virginia fire code.
