Stone Masonry Paving — Richmond VA & Central Virginia

Stone masonry paving is the pinnacle of outdoor hardscaping — materials with centuries of proven durability that only improve with age. J. Worden & Sons brings decades of stone masonry expertise to Richmond VA homeowners, with completed projects in Tuckahoe, Windsor Farms, Westham Parkway, Sleepy Hollow, and throughout Chesterfield and Henrico County. Our stone masonry work is documented in our Houzz portfolio, which has earned multiple Best of Houzz awards.

We work with the full range of natural stone — flagstone, bluestone, travertine, slate, fieldstone, limestone, and granite — as well as high-quality manufactured stone pavers. Each project begins with a design consultation to select materials and develop a layout that integrates with the home's architecture. Our installation is meticulous: proper excavation and base preparation, precision stone cutting, careful joint treatment that locks the installation and prevents future shifting.

Stone Masonry Paving — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does stone masonry paving cost in Richmond VA?

$20–$50 per sq ft depending on stone type and complexity. Natural flagstone and bluestone are at the lower end; custom granite and complex patterns at the higher end.

What types of stone do you work with?

Flagstone, bluestone, travertine, slate, fieldstone, limestone, granite, and manufactured stone pavers.

How long does stone masonry last?

50–100+ years properly installed. Natural stone is essentially indestructible — the installation determines longevity.

Can stone masonry be combined with asphalt?

Yes — a very popular approach. Asphalt main driveway with stone entry apron, walkway, or courtyard. One contractor for the entire project.

Do you serve Tuckahoe, Windsor Farms, and Westham Parkway?

Yes — these are among our most active stone masonry areas. Our Houzz portfolio includes completed projects from these neighborhoods.
