Asphalt Paving Cost Guide — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does asphalt driveway paving cost in Virginia in 2025?

$3–$7 per sq ft installed. A 2-car driveway (600 sq ft) costs $1,800–$4,200. A 3-car driveway (900 sq ft) runs $2,700–$6,300. Prices vary with excavation depth, soil conditions, and site access.

What factors affect paving cost in Virginia?

Project size (larger = lower per-sq-ft), excavation depth, existing material removal, drainage requirements, site access for equipment, and current asphalt material prices.

How much does sealcoating cost in Richmond VA?

$0.15–$0.25 per sq ft. A 600 sq ft driveway costs $90–$150. A 2,000 sq ft parking lot runs $300–$500. Crack filling adds $2–$4 per linear foot.

How much does parking lot paving cost for a commercial property?

$2–$5 per sq ft for new construction, $1.50–$3.50 for resurfacing. A 50-space lot (~15,000 sq ft) runs $30,000–$75,000 for new build. ADA, drainage, and striping are additional.

Why are some paving quotes so much lower?

Low-ball quotes almost always involve shortcuts: inadequate base depth, thin asphalt, skipping edge work, or inferior materials. The base preparation is where you get cheated most — it's underground and invisible. Always ask for excavation depth and base specification in writing.
