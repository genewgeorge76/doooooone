Why Asphalt for Sleepy Hollow Estates?

Asphalt's rich black finish is the luxury choice for prestigious estates. It complements architectural beauty, enhances curb appeal, and when properly maintained, develops a distinguished patina. Sleepy Hollow's clay soils and seasonal cycles demand flexible pavement—asphalt is the superior choice.
