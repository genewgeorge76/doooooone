Asphalt Paving Richmond VA — FAQs

How much does asphalt paving cost in Richmond VA?

Asphalt driveway paving in Richmond VA runs $3–$7 per square foot. A standard 2-car driveway (600–800 sq ft) typically costs $2,500–$6,000 depending on excavation depth, base thickness, and site access. Commercial parking lots run $2–$5 per sq ft. All estimates are free, written, and delivered after an in-person site visit. Call (804) 446-1296.

How long does asphalt last in Richmond's climate?

A properly installed asphalt driveway in Richmond VA lasts 20–30 years with regular sealcoating every 3–5 years. Richmond's clay soils and seasonal freeze-thaw cycles require solid base preparation — we excavate to 8–10 inches and install a compacted aggregate base on every job.

What is the best time of year to pave in Richmond VA?

The ideal paving season in Richmond runs April through October when temps stay above 50°F. Spring and early fall are the sweet spots — moderate temps, lower humidity, and stable ground. We book up fast in May–June, so schedule your free estimate early.

Do you serve all Richmond VA zip codes?

Yes — we serve all Richmond zip codes including 23221, 23220, 23222–23232, and surrounding areas. We're based in Chester (15 min south on I-95) and serve the full city and surrounding Chesterfield and Henrico counties.

Are you licensed and insured in Richmond VA?

Yes. J. Worden & Sons holds a Virginia Class A Contractor License and carries full general liability and workers' compensation insurance. We've maintained active licensure since 1984. Proof of insurance available on request before any job begins.
