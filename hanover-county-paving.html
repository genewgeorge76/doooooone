Hanover County VA's Trusted Asphalt Paving Contractor

Hanover County is one of the Richmond metro area's most dynamic jurisdictions — spanning everything from the charming college town of Ashland (home to Randolph-Macon College) to the dense suburban neighborhoods of Mechanicsville and Atlee, and north through rural farming communities to Doswell and Beaverdam. This geographic variety means an equally diverse range of paving needs: suburban driveways in established Atlee subdivisions that are due for replacement, commercial parking lots along the Route 1 corridor that need resurfacing, and rural driveways in northern Hanover that might be candidates for chip-and-tar. J. Worden & Sons handles them all, and at just 25 miles from our Chester VA base, Hanover County is right in our service heartland.

Hanover County's soils create interesting variation. The western half of the county — including Ashland and Montpelier — sits on Piedmont red clay, the same expansive, moisture-retaining soil that requires careful excavation and drainage engineering found in Chesterfield and Henrico. The eastern half, toward the New Kent County border, transitions into Coastal Plain sandy soils that drain better but offer less stable subgrade without proper aggregate base depth. The suburban Mechanicsville and Atlee areas have heavy clay soils that are among the most demanding for asphalt longevity. Our crew knows Hanover's soils well and designs each project's base preparation to match the specific conditions of the site.

Asphalt Paving Hanover County VA — FAQs

How much does asphalt paving cost in Hanover County VA?

Asphalt paving in Hanover County VA runs $3–$7 per square foot for residential driveways. A standard two-car driveway (600–800 sq ft) typically costs $2,500–$6,000. Commercial parking lots are quoted individually by scope. We travel from Chester VA — just 25 miles — with no travel surcharge for Hanover County projects. All estimates are free, written, and in-person. Call (804) 446-1296 to schedule your site visit.

Do you serve all of Hanover County including Ashland, Mechanicsville, and Atlee?

Yes — we serve the entire county including Ashland, Mechanicsville, Atlee, Montpelier, Beaverdam, Hanover Courthouse, Cold Harbor area, Doswell, and all surrounding communities. ZIP codes served include 23005 (Ashland), 23111 and 23116 (Mechanicsville/Atlee), and 23060 (Glen Allen border area). Hanover County is close to our Chester base and we know the communities throughout the county well.

How does Hanover County's mix of clay and sandy soils affect paving?

Hanover County spans the Piedmont-to-Coastal-Plain transition. Western areas near Ashland and Montpelier have more Piedmont red clay — expansive soils requiring deeper excavation and drainage attention. Eastern areas near the New Kent border shift toward sandier Coastal Plain soils that drain better but need proper aggregate base depth. Mechanicsville's dense suburban areas have heavy clay soils. We assess each site's specific soil profile and design the base preparation accordingly for lasting results.

What's the typical turnaround time for a driveway project in Hanover County?

A standard driveway replacement in Hanover County takes 1–2 days from start to finish. Estimate scheduling is typically within 1–2 weeks, and we can often begin projects within 2–4 weeks of estimate approval depending on season. Spring and summer book fastest — call in late winter if you want a spring installation. We provide a complete project timeline with your written estimate so you can plan around it.

Can you handle commercial paving along Hanover County's Route 1 corridor?

Yes — we handle commercial parking lots, driveways, and paved surfaces along Hanover County's Route 1 corridor from Ashland south through Chamberlayne and surrounding commercial zones. Commercial projects include full parking lot installation, ADA-compliant striping, drainage engineering, and phased construction to minimize business disruption. The Route 1 corridor is an active commercial area we know well. Contact us for a commercial site evaluation and free estimate.
