Driveway Paving Cost in Virginia Beach

Typical pricing: $3–$7 per square foot installed. A standard 2-car driveway costs $2,000–$4,500. All estimates are free and in-person.

Why Asphalt? Coastal Considerations

Virginia Beach's coastal environment presents unique challenges: salt spray, high humidity, freeze-thaw cycles, and sand accumulation. Asphalt's flexibility handles these stresses better than rigid concrete. Combined with our salt-resistant sealcoating, your driveway will maintain its integrity for 20–30 years.

The Driveway Installation Process

Step 1: Free On-Site Consultation

We evaluate existing conditions, account for coastal water drainage, and provide a written estimate.

Step 2: Site Preparation & Excavation

8–10 inch excavation accounting for Virginia Beach's water table and sand infiltration.

Step 3: Base Installation & Compaction

4–6 inches of compacted crushed stone provides the foundation that lasts.

Step 4: Asphalt Application

Premium hot-mix asphalt at ~300°F, 2–3 inches for driveways.

Step 5: Rolling & Compaction

Heavy rollers create a smooth, durable, salt-resistant surface.

Step 6: Final Cleanup

Inspect, walkthrough, complete site cleanup.

Driveway Lifespan & Salt-Air Maintenance

Expect 20–30 years with proper care. Virginia Beach driveways need:

  • Sealcoating: Every 2–3 years (more frequently due to salt spray)
  • Rinse after winter: Hose off salt residue
  • Crack sealing: Address cracks immediately
  • Drainage maintenance: Keep gutters clear
