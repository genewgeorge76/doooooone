Midlothian VA's Trusted Paving Contractor

Midlothian, VA has transformed from a rural Chesterfield community into one of the Richmond metro area's most desirable suburban destinations — and J. Worden & Sons has paved a lot of the driveways along the way. We've worked in Brandermill since it was a new development in the 1970s and '80s, watching the community mature and the original driveways reach the end of their lifecycle. We understand the drainage considerations of Swift Creek's watershed communities, the larger driveway footprints typical of Woodlake's executive homes, and the commercial demands of Midlothian Turnpike's busy business corridor.

Midlothian's clay-heavy soils and the presence of significant tree canopy in established neighborhoods like Brandermill and Harbour Pointe create specific paving challenges. Tree roots can compromise asphalt from below, and clay soils amplify the freeze-thaw damage that cracks pavement from the surface. Our approach addresses both: we excavate to proper depth, address any root intrusion before laying base material, install compacted aggregate base for structural support, and apply hot-mix asphalt that's been calibrated for Virginia's seasonal demands.

Asphalt Paving Midlothian VA — FAQs

How much does asphalt paving cost in Midlothian VA?

Asphalt driveway paving in Midlothian VA runs $3–$7 per square foot. A standard 2-car driveway typically costs $2,500–$6,000. We're based in Chester (15 min from Midlothian) so there are no travel surcharges. All estimates are free and in-person.

Do you serve Brandermill and Woodlake in Midlothian?

Yes — Brandermill and Woodlake are two of our most active Midlothian service areas. We handle driveways of all sizes throughout these communities and understand the specific drainage and soil considerations that come with properties near Swift Creek Reservoir.

How do you handle tree roots near driveways in Midlothian?

In established Midlothian neighborhoods with mature tree canopy, we assess any root intrusion during our free site visit. Depending on proximity and root size, we may recommend root barrier installation or strategic excavation to address the issue before laying base material — preventing premature cracking.

Do you offer sealcoating in Midlothian VA?

Yes — sealcoating is one of our most popular services in Midlothian. We recommend sealcoating every 3–5 years to protect against UV oxidation, water infiltration, and gas/oil spills. It's the most cost-effective way to extend your driveway's life.
