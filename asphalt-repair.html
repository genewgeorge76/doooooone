Asphalt Repair & Patching — Richmond VA & Central Virginia

Asphalt damage compounds quickly — a small crack becomes a pothole, a pothole becomes a base failure. What could have been a $200 patch becomes a $4,000 section replacement. The most cost-effective approach is early intervention: sealing cracks when they first appear, patching potholes before they expand, addressing raveling before water infiltrates the base. J. Worden & Sons provides fast, permanent asphalt repair for driveways and commercial lots throughout Richmond VA and all of Central Virginia.

We use hot-mix asphalt for structural patches — saw-cut to clean edges, excavate any base failures, restore the base, compact fresh hot-mix to match the surrounding surface. Cold-patch is available for temporary repairs. We assess each situation individually and recommend the most durable, cost-effective solution every time.

Asphalt Repair & Patching — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does asphalt repair cost in Richmond VA?

Small patches start at $150–$300. Larger structural repairs run $5–$12 per sq ft depending on depth. Free in-person estimates. Call (804) 446-1296.

Repair vs. replace — how do I decide?

If less than 25–30% of the surface has damage, repair is usually cost-effective. Widespread base failure or 20+ year old pavement often warrants full replacement. We give an honest assessment.

What causes potholes in Virginia driveways?

Cracks allow water to infiltrate the base. In winter, water freezes and expands, pushing the surface up. When ice melts, the base collapses, leaving a void under the surface.

What is alligator cracking?

Interconnected cracks indicating base failure. Requires full-depth patching — remove damaged asphalt and failed base, install fresh base, pave over with hot-mix. Surface-only treatments are temporary.

How long do asphalt repairs last?

A properly done hot-mix patch lasts 10–20 years — as long as the surrounding pavement.
