Concrete Paving — Richmond VA & Central Virginia

Concrete paving offers long lifespan, low maintenance, and visual flexibility that makes it the right choice for many Richmond VA applications. Concrete driveways last 30–40 years and resist petroleum spills that can soften asphalt surfaces. Concrete also supports a wide range of decorative treatments — brushed finishes, exposed aggregate, stamped patterns, colored concrete — giving homeowners far more aesthetic options.

J. Worden & Sons installs concrete driveways, walkways, steps, curbs, aprons, commercial slabs, and flatwork throughout Richmond VA and all of Central Virginia. Our process includes proper site preparation, excavation, base installation, form setting, concrete placement, finishing, and curing. We design control joints at proper intervals to manage cracking and ensure the finished surface looks clean and professional for decades.

Concrete Paving — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a concrete driveway cost in Richmond VA?

$6–$12 per sq ft. A 600 sq ft driveway runs $3,600–$7,200. Decorative finishes add $2–$5/sq ft.

Concrete vs. asphalt for a Richmond VA driveway?

Asphalt: lower upfront cost ($3–$7/sq ft), flexible, easier to repair. Concrete: longer lifespan (30–40 yrs), less maintenance, resists oil spills. We help you decide based on your priorities.

How long before I can drive on new concrete?

7 days for light vehicle traffic. Full cure (28 days) before heavy vehicles.

Do you install decorative concrete?

Yes — brushed, broom-finish, exposed aggregate, and stamped concrete. Colored concrete also available.
