Asphalt Paving Henrico County VA — FAQs

How much does asphalt paving cost in Henrico County VA?

Asphalt driveway paving in Henrico County runs $3–$7 per square foot. A 2-car driveway (600–800 sq ft) typically costs $2,500–$6,000. Commercial lots run $2–$5 per sq ft. All estimates are free and in-person. Call (804) 446-1296.

Do you serve Short Pump and Glen Allen in Henrico County?

Yes — Short Pump, Glen Allen, and Innsbrook are among our most active Henrico service areas. We handle both residential driveways and commercial parking lots throughout the western Henrico corridor including zip codes 23233, 23238, 23059, and 23060.

Do you serve eastern Henrico — Sandston and Highland Springs?

Yes — we serve all of Henrico County including eastern communities like Sandston (23150), Highland Springs (23075), and Varina (23231). Our crew covers the full county from a Chester base with competitive pricing across all Henrico zip codes.

How long does asphalt last in Henrico County VA?

A properly installed driveway in Henrico VA lasts 20–30 years with sealcoating every 3–5 years. Henrico's red clay soils require proper base preparation — we excavate to 8–10 inches and install compacted aggregate base on every job to ensure longevity.
