The Driveway Installation Process

Step 1: Free On-Site Consultation

Evaluate site conditions, drainage, base integrity, and provide detailed written estimate.

Step 2: Excavation & Base Prep

8–10 inch excavation accounting for Northern Virginia clay soils. Compacted crushed aggregate base.

Step 3: Premium Asphalt Application

Premium hot-mix asphalt, 2–3 inches, laid and compacted to perfection.

Step 4: Final Quality Inspection

Walkthrough, cleanup, quality assurance.

Why Asphalt? Northern Virginia Climate

Northern Virginia's freeze-thaw cycles and clay-heavy soils demand flexible pavement. Asphalt handles this movement; rigid concrete cracks. Asphalt is also 30–40% less expensive upfront and easier to repair when needed.

Driveway Lifespan & Maintenance

20–30 years with proper care:

  • Sealcoating every 3–5 years
  • Crack sealing immediately
  • Regular drainage maintenance
  • Annual inspection
