Cobblestone Paving — Richmond VA & Central Virginia

Cobblestone paving adds permanence and elegance that asphalt and concrete simply cannot match. J. Worden & Sons has extensive experience installing cobblestone driveways, walkways, courtyard surfaces, and decorative borders in Richmond VA's most distinctive neighborhoods — Sleepy Hollow, Tuckahoe, Windsor Farms, Westham Parkway, the Fan District, and throughout Chesterfield and Henrico County. Our cobblestone work appears in the Houzz portfolio that earned us multiple Best of Houzz awards.

We work with both natural cobblestone (granite, Belgian block, reclaimed historic stone) and manufactured concrete cobblestone pavers. Each project begins with a design consultation to select stone type, color, pattern, and border treatment. Installation includes full excavation, a properly prepared sand and gravel base, precise hand-setting of each stone, and polymeric sand joint filling to lock the installation and resist weed growth for decades.

Cobblestone Paving — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does cobblestone paving cost in Richmond VA?

$15–$30 per sq ft installed. Natural granite and Belgian block are at the higher end; manufactured concrete pavers are more affordable. Free in-person estimates.

How long does cobblestone last?

50–100+ years properly installed. Individual stones can be replaced if damaged — one of the most maintainable surfaces available.

What types of cobblestone do you install?

Natural granite, Belgian block, reclaimed historic cobblestone, and manufactured concrete pavers.

Can cobblestone be combined with asphalt?

Yes — very popular combination. Asphalt for the main driveway with cobblestone borders, aprons, or courtyard areas. We handle both trades.

Do you serve Sleepy Hollow, Tuckahoe, and Windsor Farms?

Yes — these are among our most active cobblestone installation areas. Our Houzz portfolio includes completed projects from these neighborhoods.
