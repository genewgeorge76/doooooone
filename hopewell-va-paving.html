How much does asphalt paving cost in Hopewell VA?

Asphalt paving in Hopewell VA typically runs $3–$7 per square foot for residential driveways. A standard two-car driveway of 600–800 square feet costs approximately $2,500–$6,000. Commercial parking lots are quoted individually by project scope. We travel from Chester VA — about 35 miles — with no travel surcharge. All estimates are free, written, and based on an in-person site visit. Call (804) 446-1296 to get started.

How does Hopewell's proximity to the Appomattox River affect paving projects?

Properties near the Appomattox River in Hopewell often sit on river bottomland soils with high clay content and moisture retention challenges. These conditions require careful drainage engineering to prevent water from pooling beneath the asphalt and accelerating deterioration. We evaluate each site's drainage profile, build in appropriate cross-slopes and perimeter drainage, and use a properly compacted aggregate base to create a stable, long-lasting surface even in Hopewell's moisture-prone areas.

Do you serve all Hopewell neighborhoods including City Point and Kingsland?

Yes — we serve all Hopewell neighborhoods, including the historic City Point waterfront area, Kingsland, Crescent Hills, the Appomattox Manor area, and downtown Hopewell. ZIP code 23860. We're experienced working in Hopewell's diverse landscape, from its historic waterfront neighborhoods to revitalized residential areas and commercial zones along Route 36 and Route 10.

How long will an asphalt driveway last in Hopewell's climate?

A properly installed asphalt driveway in Hopewell should last 20–30 years with regular maintenance. Hopewell's humid summers and freeze-thaw winters, combined with its river-influenced soils, can accelerate deterioration on surfaces with inadequate base preparation. Sealcoating every 3–4 years, prompt crack repair, and ensuring good perimeter drainage are the three most important maintenance steps. The foundation is everything — a well-built base is the single greatest factor in driveway longevity.

Can you handle both residential and industrial/commercial paving in Hopewell?

Absolutely. Hopewell's industrial heritage and diverse property mix is well within our scope. We handle single-family residential driveways, industrial access roads, commercial parking lots, and large multi-phase commercial paving projects. Our equipment and crew are sized to handle major commercial work efficiently, and we can phase work to minimize disruption to ongoing business operations.
