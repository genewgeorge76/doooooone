Driveway Paving Services We Offer

New Asphalt Driveway Installation

A new driveway is a major investment in your home's curb appeal and value. Our process includes:

  • Site inspection and soil assessment
  • Proper grading and base preparation
  • Professional asphalt laying at the correct temperature
  • Final compaction and finishing

How long does a new driveway last? With proper installation and regular sealcoating, 15–20 years or more.

Driveway Repair & Pothole Patching

Potholes, cracks, and settling are signs your driveway needs attention. We repair quickly and effectively:

  • Pothole filling and compaction
  • Crack filling and sealing
  • Partial resurfacing for damaged sections
  • Full driveway restoration

Driveway Sealcoating & Maintenance

Sealcoating is the best way to extend your driveway's life. It protects against water damage, UV rays, and chemical spills. Recommended every 2–3 years.
